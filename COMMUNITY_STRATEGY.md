## Ambassador Program

### Overview
**Goal**: Turn best users into advocates who recruit, test features, and evangelize

**Benefits**:
- Lifetime 50% discount
- Early access to features
- Swag & recognition
- Monthly stipend ($50-200)
- Direct access to team

### Tier Structure

#### Tier 1: Brand Ambassadors (5-10 people)
**Criteria**:
- 5,000+ followers on Twitter/LinkedIn
- Active in developer communities
- Regular public speaking/content
- Strong alignment with TokMan values

**Benefits**:
- $200/month stipend
- Exclusive swag (hoodies, stickers)
- Speaking opportunity slots
- Direct Slack with team
- Feature request priority

**Responsibilities**:
- 2 tweets/month mentioning TokMan
- 1 blog post/quarter (optional)
- Feedback on major releases
- Network introductions

#### Tier 2: Community Champions (20-30 people)
**Criteria**:
- 500+ followers or strong community presence
- Active beta users (10+ files analyzed)
- Regular Discord participation
- Positive feedback and constructive criticism

**Benefits**:
- $100/month stipend
- Monthly swag (stickers, merch)
- Featured in community spotlight
- Early access to beta features
- Exclusive Discord channel

**Responsibilities**:
- 1 tweet/month with #TokMan
- Help answer questions in #help
- Test beta features and provide feedback
- Monthly community check-in call

#### Tier 3: Community Helpers (50+ people)
**Criteria**:
- Regular Discord participants
- 5+ answers in #help channel
- Positive community sentiment
- Any follower count

**Benefits**:
- $25/month stipend (optional)
- Monthly sticker pack
- "Community Helper" badge
- Recognition in #announcements
- Discord moderator perks

**Responsibilities**:
- Help answer questions in #help
- Share feature feedback
- Recruit 3+ new users
- Attend monthly community call

### Recruitment Process

#### Month 1: Identify Candidates

## Community Guidelines

### Core Values
1. **Respectful**: All backgrounds and experience levels welcome
2. **Helpful**: Share knowledge generously
3. **Honest**: Constructive criticism valued
4. **Inclusive**: No discrimination or harassment
5. **Safe**: No spam, self-promotion spam, or malicious content

### Code of Conduct
```
✅ DO:
- Ask questions
- Share learnings
- Give feedback
- Help others
- Celebrate wins
- Report issues

❌ DON'T:
- Spam or self-promote
- Harass or discriminate
- Share confidential info
- Post malware/exploits
- Flame or troll
- Cross-post everywhere
```

### Moderation Policy
- **First violation**: Warning in DM
- **Second violation**: Temporary mute (24 hours)
- **Third violation**: Kick from server
- **Severe violations**: Immediate ban
